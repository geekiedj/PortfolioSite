About Me

I am a software developer with a background in Business Management & Marketing. I combine my skills from different walks of life as a business development associate / a business owner / a DJ and my passion for technology to make my ideas come to life. I've always been a creator. From playing and mixing songs, managing my business, or typing lines of code on an IDE, I love creating and solving problems that is my passion. I am a product based software developer, with an eye for creativity and design capable of solving real world problems with programming languages & technologies. I'm a business-minded, idea-driven individual passionate about building innovative solutions with great teams that contribute directly or indirectly to the betterment of Africa and the world at large. I work very well within a team and strive to be reliable and consistent in the work I deliver, and to a high standard.
